CAREER STRATEGIES

STEPS OF WRITING GOOD CURRICULUM VITAE

1. Personal Information: full name, address, date of birth, mobile number, place of birth, town, LGA, state, country
2. Experience: Position, Place and Duration
3. Professional Education: School, Place, Qualification, Grade and Duration
4. Certifications
5. Advanced Skills and Knowledge Areas
6. Interest
7. References if needed

INTERVIEW TIPS
  • Make intensive Research to know more about the Company you are scheduled to have an interview with.
  • Dress smart and official; First impression matters a lot, thus you will be addressed with the way your dressed.
  • Improve your interview techniques.
  • Always prepare for an emergency phone interview.
  • Take time to review typical interview questions, it will give you a framework for your response to the interviewer and also calm your nerves..
  • Knowing and avoiding most common interview mistakes will be an added advantage.
  • Always arrive earlier than scheduled time at the interview venue.
  • Try to comport yourself during the interview session as regards to your body language.
  • Give your answers direct and straight to the point, avoid losing focus.
  • Always apply courtesy by saying Please, Excuse me. And thank you etc.
