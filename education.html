Solomon Max Education

In a bid to continuously perfect support for growing client and partner needs, Solomon Max unveils – Solomon Max Education as part of our offering.

Solomon Max Ltd through its growing alliances with International training institutions now enhances its Human Development offerings by working with institutions and training partners around the world to offer all individuals, from school leavers to Chief Executives, the opportunity to get the best education worldwide.

We latch them to these institutions for foundation, BSc, MSC, PHD, Executive trainings, emotional intelligence, Personal effectiveness trainings and industry specific tailored modules, to mention a few.

We also provide relevant career guidance for our clients taking into consideration their previous qualifications, family and financial background, career interests and future goals to assist them in choosing the right course and academic institution.

We support our clients through the whole application process, visa counselling and application, even to getting suitable accommodation for them in their chosen overseas country.

We go further by ensuring strategic follow up while they are on course.

EMPLOYMENT DATA LATCH

On completion of their courses we retrain them on basics that relate to their need, e.g. job search fundamentals for fresh graduates, we then add clients onto our growing HR database to make them more visible for subsequent employment locally and internationally and present a holistic human development package.

This is the Solomon Max Human Development bond that ties knowledge to productivity in workspaces.
